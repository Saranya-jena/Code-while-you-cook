Task :

Imagine a situation.. you were enjoying chatting with your neighbours but suddenly some of you relatives came and demanded to have kheer immedietly as they were tired and hungry.As soon as you started making kheer you realised there is no sugar in your home. You got panicked and went to your neighbour's home for sugar.But since you were in a hurry instead of asking for sugar you asked for salt.But since your neighbour is not a mind reader she gives you the salt container.

Now stop...imagining



And write down your reasons why was not she able to understand you, why did not she think of giving sugar instead?




Answer

It is because when you asked for salt, she knows what does salt actually mean, it has fixed meaning and you can't change it even if you want. Similarly in programming language also there is term "Keywords" which are the words that convey a special meaning to the language compiler. You can't use it anywhere in the code in a different context.
